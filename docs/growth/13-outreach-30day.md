# 30-Day Backlink & Distribution Outreach

**Principle:** Value first. Pitch original tables, methodology, or tool improvements — never “please link to us because SEO.”  
**Primary assets to offer:**
- Coast by age tables (+ CSV export on tool)
- Free sequence stress-test (transparent model)
- Scenario A/B compare
- Coast assumptions checklist (`/resources/coast-fire-checklist`)
- Pillar posts (Coast + SOR, Barista vs Coast, why simple calcs fail)

```
Hi [Name] —

I built free Coast FIRE age tables (with 5% and 4% real columns) and a CSV export
so readers can re-run numbers under stated assumptions.

Happy to let you reuse the table with attribution, or I can generate a custom
spend level for a post you’re writing.

Live tool: https://retirefire.net/calculators/coast-fire
Article: https://retirefire.net/blog/coast-fire-by-age-tables

No paywall on the core tools. Educational only.

[Your name]
```

## 30-day cadence

| Week | Focus |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Publish assets live; 5 personalized pitches (newsletters + Coast blogs) |
| 2 | 5 more pitches; 3 genuine community answers; track replies |
| 3 | Follow-ups; 1 guest table offer; podcast/show-note pitches |
| 4 | Roundup sites; recap what converted; double down |
