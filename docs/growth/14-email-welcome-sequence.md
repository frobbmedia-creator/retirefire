## Email 2 — Day 2

**Subject:** Coast FIRE ≠ retire tomorrow

```
A common mix-up:

Coast FIRE asks whether growth *may* finish your full FIRE target by a
traditional retirement age if you stop contributing.

It does *not* mean early retirement is funded this year.

Barista FIRE is a different question — work income covers part of spend
so the portfolio only funds the gap.

Side-by-side:
https://retirefire.net/blog/barista-fire-vs-coast-fire

Compare both tools with the same spending assumption:
https://retirefire.net/calculators/coast-fire
https://retirefire.net/calculators/barista-fire

— RetireFire
```
